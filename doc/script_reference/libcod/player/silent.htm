silent( <mode> )

Module: Player

Summary:

Enables/disables some sounds that a player emits during gameplay. Further notes:

  • Hides footstep, landing, jumping, and foliage sounds for the player so that other players cannot hear them
  • Other players can still hear melee swing & miss sounds if they are close enough
  • Silenced players can still hear some of their own actions that others cannot hear (e.g., landing, gear rattle)



Call this on:

The player



Example:

self silent("on");



Required Args:

  • 1 : <mode> The mode to switch to: "on", "off", or "toggle".
